This tool is an educational demonstration built on two public research datasets. It is not a medical device and does not provide a diagnosis. Risk factors for heart disease are numerous and interrelated — always discuss personal risk with a qualified clinician.
DATA — heart.csv (Cleveland-derived clinical dataset, UCI-style encoding, 1,025 records) and framingham.csv (Framingham Heart Study export, 4,240 records, 10-year CHD outcome). Models are logistic regressions fit in-session on these files; missing Framingham lab values were median-imputed. Built for exploratory, educational use only.
